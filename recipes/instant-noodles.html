Instant Noodles

Description

Craving a quick and satisfying meal? This instant noodles recipe transforms a simple packet of noodles into a flavorful, comforting dish in just minutes. With a savory broth, vibrant vegetables, and your choice of protein or egg, it’s the perfect balance of convenience and taste. Whether you're in a rush or just want an easy upgrade to your favorite snack, this recipe delivers a delicious, homemade twist on classic instant noodles—warm, hearty, and irresistibly slurpable.

ingredients

  • Instant Noodle
  • Wamter
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Open noodles
  2. Boil Wamter
  3. Add noodles in Wamter
  4. Add flavor packet
  5. Wait 5min
  6. Let cool and enjoy!
