Lasagna

Description

This hearty lasagna layers rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and gooey cheese between tender pasta sheets for the ultimate comfort food. Baked to golden perfection, it’s a classic dish that’s perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Make sauce using onion, Garlic and Crushed tomatoes
  2. Cook beef
  3. Add sauce to beef
  4. Cook pasta
  5. Layer pasta and beef
  6. Add cheese on top
  7. Bake for five minutes to melt cheese
  8. Enjoy
