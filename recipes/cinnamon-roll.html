Cinnamon Rolls

Description

Indulge in the warm, comforting aroma of freshly baked cinnamon rolls with this classic homemade recipe. Soft, fluffy dough spirals are filled with a rich cinnamon-sugar blend, then baked to golden perfection and topped with a smooth, creamy glaze that melts into every swirl. Perfect for a cozy breakfast, weekend brunch, or a sweet treat any time of day, these cinnamon rolls strike the perfect balance between gooey and tender. With simple ingredients and step-by-step instructions, this recipe brings bakery-style results right to your kitchen.

ingredients

  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Cinnamon
  • Sugar
  • Yeast

step-by-step

  1. Mix the dry ingredients
  2. Activate the Yeast
  3. Add wet ingredients into the mix
  4. Roll the dough until it's no longer sticky
  5. Rest the dough until it doubles in size
  6. Roll the dough into a rectangle and add the cinnamon
  7. Make the cinnamon roll shape and cut into slices
  8. Let them rest for 20min
  9. Bake for 20min at 200 Celsius
  10. Enjoy yummy rolls
